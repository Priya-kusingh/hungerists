From creamy Kaju katli for Diwali to mouth-watering Gujiyan for Holi, and delightful Modaks for Ganesh Chaturthi, festivals are bound with yummy sweets.

Therefore, HUNGERIST Just Pure has come up with the best organic delicacies that are not just full of taste but nutrition. These traditional treats get the natural sweetness either from dry fruits/nuts or through organic Sulphur-free sugar and combined with the finest quality of ingredients which are carefully sourced to ensure there won’t be any difference in taste but definitely in the quality.

We have a wide range of freshly and hygienically made organic sweets which you can easily order online and get delivered to your doorstep in Delhi/NCR

Why OMSWEETS Just Pure?

Be it kids, adults, or the elderly, sweet cravings are normal. We have the best sweets lined up for you and your family, and that can be easily ordered online within a few clicks:

  • Our sweets are 100% pure, organic, and chemical-free.
  • We use organic ingredients to prepare a variety of sweets.
  • We use A2/A2 cow milk from OMDAIRY Just Pure, Mawa or Ghee is extracted from our A2/A2 cow milk.
  • We use organic- sulphur-free sugar, dry fruits & nuts, or jaggery to add sweetness without the usage of refined sugar.
  • We don’t keep stock. Our sweets are freshly prepared to order and delivered fresh to ensure taste, quality, and freshness.
  • We have strict guidelines when it comes to carefully sourcing and picking the ingredients.
  • We use sustainable packaging for sweet boxes to ensure safety for the planet as a whole.

Our Organic Ingredients

We use the finest quality of ingredients which makes our sweets completely irresistible. Here are a few of them:

  • Mamra Almonds– Crunchier, crispier, and boast a higher nutrition value and oil in comparison
  • Cashew (W240) – The ‘King of Cashews’ due to their superior grade quality and contain the highest amount of nutrition.
  • Afghani Anjeer – Premium quality Afghani Anjeer or Figs without any artificial flavour & preservatives and offer rich taste, purity, and longer shelf-life
  • Kishori Pistachio– Delicious and nutritious nut brimming with healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants.
  • Pure 99.99% Silver Vark/Chandi Vark coating– Our Silver Vark is handpicked from our own goldsmith to ensure the no-mixing approach.
  • A2/A2 Cow Milk – Dairy products (Mava, and Ghee) come from OMDAIRY Just Pure and are made from A2/A2 Cow milk.
  • Sulphur-free sugar – It is safer and healthier than other processed sugar.
