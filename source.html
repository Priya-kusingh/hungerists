Sourcing & Process

Taste & Trust in Every Step!

Farmer’s Association

In a short amount of time we have been able to cultivate a close network of over 500 certified small and marginal farmers directly or indirectly through our partner organizations, farmer groups, self-help groups, and women cooperatives across the country.

Self-Growing Facility

For us, sustainability is an expression of our conscious endeavor to live responsibly, conserve, and eat well to enjoy a joyful and wholesome life. Our proposed Organic Farms located in Nainital, Almora and Noida would soon be ready for sourcing Organic Produce exclusively for OMKITCHEN. This means we can further control the quality and reduce the time and effort in bringing you quality meals whenever you require. This initiative would help us attain self-sufficiency in providing Farm-to-Plate food solution and aid local farmers develop confidence and expertise with our farms as a model.

Process

We follow strict organic certification guidelines for the selection of farmers in our vendor list. A total of 127 pesticides (FSSAI 2018 notification) are tested in each product we buy and include in your food.

Our dedicated team of sourcing specialists scout for the best organic produce all over the country. We source seasonal, locally-grown, organic products.

We get samples and lots tested in labs for their organic purity. 127 pesticides are tested in certified labs.

Our raw material scheduling and in-house storage system ensures the produce stays fresh and is utilized within the prescribed freshness period.
